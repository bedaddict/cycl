About

Cycl. is your trusted companion for managing your health and well-being. Designed for women, Cycl. offers an all-in-one solution to track your menstrual cycle, monitor your health, and connect with professionals for personalized advice. Whether you want to plan ahead, understand your body's unique rhythm, or seek expert guidance, Cycl. is here to support you every step of the way.

What Cycl. Offers:

  • Moon Cycle: Discover the connection between your cycle and lunar phases for a holistic view of your health.
  • Symptom Patterns: Identify trends in your symptoms to better understand your body's signals.
  • Secret Chat: Consult with professional without getting disturb.

At Cycl. we believe that understanding your body is empowering. Our mission is to help you take control of your health with confidence and ease.

Moon Cycle

Connect with Your Natural Rhythms.

Understanding the connection between your menstrual cycle and the lunar phases can enhance your awareness of your body's natural rhythms. This feature allows you to:

  • Visualize the Lunar Phases: See how your menstrual cycle corresponds to the waxing and waning of the moon.
  • Track Mood and Energy Levels: Log your emotional and physical states throughout your cycle, helping you identify patterns tied to lunar changes.
  • Gain Insight into Your Natural Rhythms: Harness the wisdom of the moon’s cycles to support your personal wellness journey and make decisions that align with your body's natural ebb and flow.

Symptom Patterns

Uncover Patterns in Your Health

Tracking recurring symptoms is essential for understanding your health. Cycl. helps you identify the root causes and patterns of symptoms, enabling you to manage them more effectively. Here's how this feature helps:

  • Track Recurring Symptoms: Log symptoms like cramps, bloating, fatigue, or headaches, and track how often they occur and when they are most intense.
  • Analyze Symptom Trends: View charts and reports that highlight when symptoms are most frequent and what might trigger them, allowing you to make proactive health decisions.
  • Personalized Action Plans: Based on your symptom patterns, Cycl. provides tailored tips, such as diet adjustments, exercise recommendations, or relaxation techniques to manage your symptoms more effectively..

Daily Insights

Tailored Tips for Every Day

With Cycl. you receive personalized insights every day based on your daily entries. These insights are designed to help you stay informed about your health, promote well-being, and enhance your day-to-day life.

Whether you’re looking for health tips that receive actionable advice on nutrition, stress management techniques that control your mood and energy movement, or daily self-care reminders tips for better sleep, Cycl. offers practical suggestions to help you thrive.

  • Health Tips: Receive actionable advice on nutrition, exercise, and self-care that is tailored to your current health status and cycle phase.
  • Mood and Energy Management: Get personalized tips for managing energy fluctuations, mood changes, and emotional health based on your cycle’s stage.
  • Daily Self-Care Reminders: Cycl. will prompt you with small but impactful actions like hydration reminders, mindfulness exercises, or relaxation techniques that support your well-being.
